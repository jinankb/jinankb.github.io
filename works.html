Work with Artisans

From 1993 till about 2006 I have been working with potters of Aruvacode village learning/ imbibing the being-ness of non-literate people and at the same time trying to revitalize the dying tradition of pottery.
http://www.re-cognition.org/crafts/index.html

The basic approach in working with artisan is to enable them to create without any inputs. See detailed philosophy at
http://www.re-cognition.org/crafts/philosophy.html

The women potters have been developing unique times and murals and this can be seen at
http://www.re-cognition.org/crafts/coiltiles.html
