🙶De Academizing Knowledge. De Institutionalizing Learning

About Me

I am a victim of modern education, uprooted from one’s authentic beingness and cultural sensibilities. Have been living and working with sense literate people (the so called illiterates) to understand natural cognitive processes and how children/ adults learn in non-coercive, natural environments. Have been studying the cognitive damages of modern schooling and also the biological roots of beauty and cognition at the university of life. Struggling to undo the damages of a formal design education, engineering degree, and schooling. At present involved in exploring how to reorganize our space so as to let children be and exploring how to undo the cognitive damages of schooling.

Children

Children are the unit of human life. Knowing and being can be understood only by revisiting some of the fundamental aspects of life- why, what and how children learn! What children learn, left to them, is the way the world is. So child learns the way the world looks, the quality of its materiality and the functions, processes and the phenomenon that happens around them. The holistic nature of the world awakens the holistic nature in the child.

Being in Beauty

Beauty needs to be understood as the very organizing principle of life, outside the paradigm of performance and appreciation. Beauty is one of the most fundamental of human existence and it is rooted in our biology just like hunger. It is beauty that dictates the rhythm, organization, love, innocence, wonder and awe. The beauty that can't be owned, controlled, manipulated as self expression but flows through you and makes you into a conduit for beauty to happen.

Being academy

The Being Academy is being imagined/ evolved/ designed to create condition for regaining our capacity to use senses, to experience the world, to be present, to regain the wonder, innocence, trust, the playfulness, to be spontaneous, genuine and authentic, to be in awe with the world to be in the world.

Re imagining schools

Schools needs to be re imagined as a space for creating knowledge instead of transferring information, developing qualities needed to create knowledge instead of memorizing and analyzing readymade knowledge, developing love, care, courage, collaboration instead of timidity, competition. Dealing with readymade knowledge creates learned minds instead of learning mind.

Tradition/Indigenity

Learning in tradition is experientially rooted, learner driven. It has the quality of re-creating, re-inventing and re-living knowledge. The cognitive space ensures the first handedness in these learnings and helps the learner to situate oneself in the cultural conditions of one’s life. Nature as the basic cognitive source also instills sustainability and harmony as the way of being and makes children the basis for organizing space.

Foundation for learning

Foundation program helps the ‘student’ who has come from teacher taught paradigm to become a ‘learner’ (self initiated, experience based, discovery dependent). Learning the real world demands abilities, attitudes and skills that have not been developed in the regular education. Foundation program is designed to awaken the child like qualities and would be some sort of a self initiated re birth.

existential knowledge foundation

Existential knowledge foundation has been set up to understand both existence and knowledge- the eternal, universal and immediate. It is an endeavor to know the perceptual and behavioral; the idea and its practice. This is a space for re-connecting with the existential aspects of knowing and being; how do we know and how do we be- the aesthetic, ethical and knowledge dimensions.

Re-cognition

‘Modern’ crisis is a crisis in cognition. The most misunderstood aspects of modernity are children and knowledge. Preoccupation with knowledge has made human beings into 'human knowings'. The totality of 'Being in the world' is an integral 'act' involving ethics, beauty and knowledge. Mediated knowing has distorted the biologically rooted cognitive system and alienated modern man from his essential nature. So a kind of re cognition is called for to reclaim his biological roots and to reconnect with himself which is nature itself.
